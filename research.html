Selected work in document intelligence, visual grounding, and vision-language models.

M3Grounder: Mask-Based Multi-Span and Multi-Granular Grounding for Document QA
Venkata Kesav Venna, Sai Madhusudan Gunda, Jyothi Swaroopa Jinka, Hrithik Sagar Rachakonda, Anirudh Srinivasan, Ravi Kiran Sarvadevabhatla
CVPR, 2026, pp. 23685-23695
description

M3Grounder studies document question answering with a focus on multi-span and multi-granular grounding, moving toward document systems that can not only answer questions but also precisely highlight supporting evidence in the document.
