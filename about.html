About

I am a Computer Vision Research Engineer at BharatGen, where I work on building vision-language foundation models and applications for document intelligence.

My interests include OCR, VQA, layout understanding, visual grounding, multilingual document AI, and modular multimodal systems that combine strong vision encoders, connectors, and language decoders in a plug-and-play fashion.
