About Me!

I am a Senior Research Engineer and Vision-Language Models Team Lead at BharatGen. I spend most of my time building multimodal and multilingual foundation models and agents for Indian document intelligence: models that can read, parse, reason over, ground, reconstruct, and evaluate documents across languages and domains.

My work sits at the intersection of vision-language modeling, document understanding, visual grounding, layout analysis, synthetic data generation, and scalable VLM training systems. At BharatGen, I helped build Patram, India's first VLM for document understanding, along with Patram-Layout, Patram-Grounder, Patram-Parse, Patram-Bench, Patram-Core, Patram-Ingest, Patram-FS, Patram-DB, DarshinAI, and e-vikrAI. My research has appeared at venues including CVPR and ICDAR. I have been fortunate to be mentored by Dr. Ravi Kiran Sarvadevabhatla and Dr. Ganesh Ramakrishnan.

I graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad with a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering. I received the Institute Gold Medal for Excellence in Academics and Co-curricular Activities, ranking 1st out of 1103 students.

As an undergraduate, I secured 1st place globally in the IEEE Signal Processing Cup 2024 through ROBOVOX, a far-field speaker recognition system for noisy real-world conditions, under Dr. Sri Rama Murty Kodukula; explored quantum methods for the Vehicle Routing Problem with q-means, D-Wave CQM, and QUBO formulations under Dr. M.V. Panduranga Rao; built LiDAR semantic segmentation pipelines for autonomous driving under Dr. C. Krishna Mohan; developed neuro-fuzzy clustering methods for Gaussian Mixture Models under Dr. Kishalay Mitra; studied adversarial robustness for IR2Vec compiler embeddings under Dr. Ramakrishna Upadrasta; and explored neuromorphic AI systems and MotionGPT for yoga postures under Dr. Mohan Raghavan.

I love giving back to the community in whatever way I can, especially through teaching, mentoring, and reviewing research. At IIT Hyderabad, I served as Head Teaching Assistant for Data Structures and Teaching Assistant for Introduction to Programming. I also mentored students through Epoch, the AI-ML and Data Science Club, and the Sunshine UG mentorship program. More recently, I have reviewed work in document layout analysis, document reconstruction, and VLM benchmarks for ICDAR and AAAI.

Inter-IIT has also been a big part of my undergraduate life. I represented IIT Hyderabad at Inter IIT Tech Meet 10.0 and 11.0, winning silver medals in the Bosch and ISRO mid-prep events, and served as Deputy Contingent Leader for the IIT Hyderabad contingent. Those weekends were some of the most intense and memorable ones I have had: fast problem solving, late-night debugging, and being surrounded by people who really cared about building things well.
