About

I am a Senior Research Engineer at BharatGen, where I lead the Vision-Language Models team, building multimodal and multilingual foundation models and agents for India.

My work spans document intelligence, OCR, VQA, layout understanding, visual grounding, multilingual data pipelines, synthetic data generation, and evaluation benchmarks. My research has been published at top venues including CVPR and ICDAR.

I am also intrigued by Neuromorphic AI and Quantum Machine Learning.

Selected Work

My current work includes Patram-Layout for promptable open-set hierarchical document segmentation, Patram-Grounder for grounded DocVQA with pixel-level masks, Patram-Parse for high-fidelity document reconstruction, Patram-Core for distributed VLM foundation-model training, Patram-Ingest for large-scale LLM/VLM data generation, and Patram-FS for high-throughput dataset access.

Earlier at BharatGen, I helped build Patram, India's first VLM for document understanding; Patram-Bench, a multilingual benchmark with 52,607 documents, 23 languages, 13 domains, 71 classes, 52 tasks, and 2.4M+ QA annotations; Patram-DB for 50M+ document images and 250M+ multimodal annotations; DarshinAI for annotation, visualization, and evaluation; and e-vikrAI for Indic e-commerce VLMs.
