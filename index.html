Anirudh /əˈnɪrʊð/ Srinivasan /sriːniˈvɑːsən/

Hi! I'm a Computer Vision Research Engineer at BharatGen, where I work on building vision-language foundation models and applications for document intelligence.

My work spans models, datasets, and benchmarks for OCR, VQA, layout understanding, visual grounding, and multilingual document AI. I am especially interested in modular multimodal systems that combine strong vision encoders, connectors, and language decoders in a plug-and-play fashion.

I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Hyderabad, where I received the Institute Gold Medal for excellence in academics and extracurricular activities.
